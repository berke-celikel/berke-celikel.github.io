<div style="text-align: center; max-width: 700px; margin: 2em auto; font-size: 1.2em; line-height: 1.6;">
  All of these shots were taken on 35mm film using my <strong>Canon F-1</strong> and <strong>Canon A-1</strong>.
  I develop the film myself, keeping the entire process <strong>fully analog</strong>—from the shutter click to the darkroom.
  <br><br>
  There’s no editing, no filters—just raw, <strong>unretouched moments</strong> captured on film.
  What you see is exactly what was shot: grain, light leaks, imperfections, and all.
